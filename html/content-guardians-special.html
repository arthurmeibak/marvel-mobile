The Guardians of the Galaxy - Holiday Special

The Guardians of the Galaxy try to settle in on the edge of the Galactic Rim. But when Kraglin, the Xandarian, shares a story of a Christmas gone wrong for Peter Quill, aka the Star-Lord, Insectoid/Celestial hybrid Mantis and Drax the Destroyer embark on a time-sensitive mission to give Peter a merrier holiday memory.
